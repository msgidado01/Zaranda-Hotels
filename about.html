About Zaranda Hotel

Located at Jos-Branch, Km 4, Bauchi, Nigeria, Zaranda Hotel is situated in one of the most beautiful and well-loved wildlife states in Nigeria.

Zaranda Hotel has a total of 184 rooms which are of different categories.
